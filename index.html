I fell in love with coding in Spring 2015. Since then I’ve been crafting my skills as front-end developer.

Born and raised in Italy, I packed my life away 16 years ago, left friends and family and began my journey on my own. The adventure started in London. I didn't know how to speak English at that point and I worked at any job that would afford me the opportunity to speak to customers non stop 8-12 hours a day. In the meanwhile I applied for grad school at SDSU, I got in and after one year I moved to California to pursue my passion for Physics. In San Diego I enjoyed working as a TA (I finally learned how to speak English! Teaching labs in a foreign language no longer scared me). After a few months in the program I was hired by Quantum Design where I worked as a Production Engineer part time while finishing my research. After I graduated they hired me full time and that was fun. In 2006 I felt there's more to life than Physics and 72 degrees sunny days. I decided it was time to move on to my next career in Translations, something I have always been passionate about, in the city of my dreams: NYC. Today, I'm a student at Codify Academy where I'm developing my front-end developer skills, and getting better at it week after week. I'm learning HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Bootstrap 3 & JavaScript. In my day job I am Italian Language Lead at TransPerfect where I manage a team of quality managers in the NY and London Production department.

When I’m not at my desk I’m most likely dancing or melting on my yoga mat, running, indoor-cycling or swimming.

If the right opportunity arises I'm available as junior front end developer. Drop me a line if you have an idea or just want to chat.
