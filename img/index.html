I fell in love with coding in Spring 2015. Since then I’ve been crafting my skills as front-end developer.

Born and raised in Italy, I moved away to see the world 16 years ago. The adventure began in London; after one year I moved to California to pursue my passion for Physics. In San Diego, the American Dream became my reality: I enjoyed working as a TA in grad school and then playing with cryogenics, superconductivity and magnetism at Quantum Design where I worked as a Production Engineer for 3 years. In 2006 I had enough of Physics and California and moved on to my next career in Translations in what I consider my home: aqui me quedo. Today, I'm a student at Codify Academy where I'm developing my front-end developer skills, and getting better at it week after week. I code HTML5, CSS3, jQuery & JavaScript. In my day job I am Italian Language Lead at TransPerfect where I manage a team of quality managers in the NY and London Production department.

When I’m not at my desk I’m most likely dancing or melting on my yoga mat, running, indoor-cycling or swimming.

If the right opportunity arises I'm available part time as junior front end developer. Drop me a line if you have an idea or just want to chat.
